Dragon Pub - Early Career Event (26 May)

The Early Career Event will be held at the Dragon Pub, also known as "kg of pasta". The cost is 15€ to be paid at the entrance and includes a drink and buffet dinner with:

  • Gramigna with ragu
  • Garganelli with speck and zucchine
  • Pasta all'arrabbiata (spicy tomato)
Other request should be made in the circulated form. To have more information on the pasta types you can have a look at: arxiv:2403.20314 and arxiv:2503.23126.

Address: Viale della Repubblica, 16a, 40127 Bologna BO, Italy

Date and Time: Monday, 26 May 2025, at 19:30

Cinema Modernissimo - Public Event (in italian, 29 May)

In the context of the ET Symposium, we organize a public outreach event on the Eintein Telescope and the future of gravitational wave science. Cinema Modernissimo is a liberty-style gem, originally opened in 1915 as part of Palazzo Ronzani, designed by architect Gualtiero Pontoni. After operating for over 90 years and closing in 2007, it was meticulously restored and reopened in November 2023.

Address: Piazza Re Enzo, 40125 Bologna BO, Italy

Date and Time: Thursday, 29 May 2025, at TBD
